# lt-110: Verify monotonicity of a toy descent step

Edit `Task.lean` only. `stepDown x grad` is a simplified nonnegative descent step on natural numbers.

Prove that one step cannot increase the iterate and that two steps cannot increase the original iterate. Do not change declaration names, theorem statements, or imports.

Forbidden constructs include `sorry`, `admit`, `axiom`, `constant`, `opaque`, `unsafe`, custom syntax or macro elaboration, `run_cmd`, `run_tac`, `Lean.addDecl`, `#eval`, and `#exit`.
