Edit `Task.lean` only. Replace the placeholder body of `PrefixSpec` with a meaningful formalization of:

`prefix` is a prefix of `xs` when `xs` can be obtained by appending some suffix to `prefix`.

Then prove the two public lemmas. You may add helper lemmas, but do not change declaration names or imports.
